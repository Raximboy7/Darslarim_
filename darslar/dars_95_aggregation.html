Aggregation (Yig'ish Funksiyalari)

Modul 14 — Database va SQL | Dars 95

Aggregate Funksiya Nima?

Aggregate (yig'ish) funksiyalar — bu ko'p qatorni olib, ulardan bitta natija qaytaradigan funksiyalar. Masalan: jadvalda 1000 ta foydalanuvchi bo'lsa, COUNT() ularning sonini — bitta raqamni qaytaradi.
Oddiy SELECT vs Aggregate

• Oddiy SELECT yosh — har bir qator uchun bitta qiymat
• SELECT AVG(yosh) — barcha qatorlardan bitta umumiy qiymat

COUNT, SUM, AVG, MIN, MAX

Faraz qilaylik, bizda users jadvali bor. Quyidagi so'rov barcha foydalanuvchilar bo'yicha yig'ma natijalarni beradi.
SELECT
    COUNT(*) AS soni,
    SUM(yosh) AS yigindi,
    AVG(yosh) AS ortacha,
    MIN(yosh) AS eng_kichik,
    MAX(yosh) AS eng_katta
FROM users;
# COUNT(*) — barcha qatorni sanaydi
# AS — natija ustuniga nom beradi
soni | yigindi | ortacha | eng_kichik | eng_katta
-----+---------+---------+------------+----------
  5  |   155   |   31.0  |    22     |    45
COUNT(*) vs COUNT(ustun)

• COUNT(*) — barcha qatorlarni sanaydi
• COUNT(email) — faqat NULL bo'lmagan email'larni sanaydi

GROUP BY — Guruhlash

GROUP BY qatorlarni guruhlarga ajratadi, so'ng har bir guruh uchun aggregate funksiyani alohida hisoblaydi. Masalan: har bir shahar bo'yicha o'rtacha yoshni topish.
SELECT shahar, AVG(yosh) AS ortacha_yosh
FROM users
GROUP BY shahar;
# Har bir shahar — alohida guruh
# Har guruh uchun AVG alohida hisoblanadi
shahar   | ortacha_yosh
---------+-------------
Toshkent | 28.5
Samarqand| 35.0
Buxoro   | 30.0
Muhim qoida

SELECT da turgan har bir oddiy ustun (aggregate emas) GROUP BY da ham bo'lishi shart. Bu yerda shahar ikkala joyda ham bor.

HAVING — Guruh Sharti

HAVING — bu guruhlarni filtrlash uchun ishlatiladi. U GROUP BY dan keyin ishlaydi va aggregate natija bo'yicha shart qo'yadi.
SELECT shahar, COUNT(*) AS soni
FROM users
GROUP BY shahar
HAVING COUNT(*) > 1;
# Faqat 1 dan ko'p odamli shaharlar qoladi
WHERE va HAVING farqi

• WHERE — guruhlashdan OLDIN, alohida qatorlarni filtrlaydi. Aggregate ishlatib bo'lmaydi.
• HAVING — guruhlashdan KEYIN, guruhlarni filtrlaydi. Aggregate ishlatish mumkin.

To'liq So'rovning Tartibi

Hamma bo'limni birga ishlatganda SQL ularni shu tartibda o'qiydi:
SELECT shahar, AVG(yosh) AS ortacha
FROM users
WHERE yosh >= 18
GROUP BY shahar
HAVING AVG(yosh) > 25
ORDER BY ortacha DESC;
# 1) WHERE — kattalarni tanlaydi
# 2) GROUP BY — shaharga bo'ladi
# 3) HAVING — o'rtacha 25 dan katta guruhlar
# 4) ORDER BY — saralash
Eslab Qoling

• COUNT/SUM/AVG/MIN/MAX — ko'p qatordan bitta natija
• GROUP BY — qatorlarni guruhlarga bo'ladi
• HAVING — guruhlarni filtrlaydi (WHERE qatorlarni)
• SELECT dagi oddiy ustun GROUP BY da ham bo'lishi shart
